Troubleshooting Tips

Backup Not Completing

  • Check your internet connection stability
  • Ensure you have enough storage space in your Carbonite plan
  • Pause and restart the backup process
  • Verify Carbonite service is running in your system tray
  • Restart your computer if issues persist

Files Not Restoring

  • Check you're logged in with the correct Carbonite account
  • Verify the files were successfully backed up previously
  • Try restoring to a different location on your computer
  • Check for any filters applied in the restore view
  • Ensure you have proper permissions on the destination folder

Carbonite Login Problems

  • Use the password recovery tool if you've forgotten credentials
  • Check your spam folder for reset emails
  • Ensure you're using the correct email address
  • Try logging in from a different browser
  • Clear browser cache and cookies if experiencing website issues

Frequently Asked Questions

What are Carbonite support hours?

Live chat support is typically available during standard business hours (9am-5pm EST, Monday-Friday). Email support operates 24/7 with responses sent during business hours. The online help center is always accessible.

Can Carbonite support help recover deleted files?

Yes, the support team can assist with file recovery from your backup history, depending on your plan's version history settings. They can help locate and restore previous versions of files.

How do I check my Carbonite backup status?

You can view backup status by logging into your Carbonite account online or checking the Carbonite application on your computer. The support team can also verify your backup status if you're experiencing issues.

What should I do if I didn't receive a response?

First check your spam folder for responses. If you still haven't received help after 48 business hours, you can follow up by replying to your original support ticket or initiating a new live chat session.

Can I access Carbonite support without an account?

Basic support resources are available to everyone, but for account-specific assistance, you'll need to verify your account ownership. The support team can help with account recovery if you've lost access.
